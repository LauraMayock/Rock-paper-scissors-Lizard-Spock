How to play

Play against the computer in the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors. Choose between Rock Paper Scissors by clicking on one of the buttons provided. the computer will randomly choose between the three choices at the same time.

The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules

Rock wins against scissors

Scissors wins against paper

Paper wins against rock
